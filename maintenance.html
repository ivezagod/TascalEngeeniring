Maintenance

It is recommended that maintenance be carried out on a regular schedule, with more frequent inspections and tests for critical equipment. The maintenance of electrical installations in a facility involves a series of activities that aim to ensure the safe and reliable operation of electrical systems and equipment within the facility. We have experienced and well-trained personnel who can handle various maintenance tasks with expertise:

Visual inspection

A visual inspection of electrical equipment is carried out to check for signs of damage, wear and tear, and other issues that may affect the safe operation of the equipment.

Testing

Electrical equipment is tested to ensure that it is operating within its design specifications. This may involve measuring voltages, currents, and other parameters to verify that they are within acceptable limits.

Replacement of parts

Parts that are worn out or damaged are replaced to ensure that the electrical equipment operates reliably.

Upgrades

Electrical equipment is upgraded to meet changing safety standards or to improve its performance.

Documentation

Records are kept of all maintenance activities, including inspections, tests, and repairs.
